Welcome to Minesweeper

How to Play

Those mines aren't going to sweep themselves!

Choose a level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.

Left-click on a square to reveal either a safe square or EGAD an exploding mine!!

Right-click, or touch and hold for 2 seconds, on a square to place a flag on it if you think it holds a mine.

You win if you have flagged all the mines correctly or revealed all safe squares.
